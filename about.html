About the Hub

Who we are, what we teach, and why practical skills come first.

Our story

Closing the skills gap, one lab at a time

The Cybersecurity Skills Hub was started by students who noticed a gap between what is taught in class and what employers in Nairobi's security operations centres actually need. We bridge that gap with short, intensive, hands-on programmes.

🎯

Our mission

To make practical cybersecurity training accessible and affordable for every student in East Africa.

👁️

Our vision

A regional talent pipeline where graduates walk into SOC and security engineering roles job-ready from day one.

🤝

Our values

  • Learn by doing
  • Ethics before exploits
  • Community over competition
